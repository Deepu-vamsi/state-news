Latest Political Developments in Andhra Pradesh

Get the latest political updates, party strategies, and key policy announcements from Andhra Pradesh.

Chief Minister Announces New Economic Policy

In a recent press conference, Andhra Pradesh Chief Minister Y.S. Jagan Mohan Reddy revealed the state's new economic policy, aimed at boosting employment and promoting industrial development.

2024 Elections: Parties Gear Up for Statewide Campaign

As the 2024 elections draw closer, political parties in Andhra Pradesh have begun ramping up their campaigns. Both the ruling and opposition parties are focusing on key issues like development and public welfare.

YSR Congress Party Announces Major Rally

The YSR Congress Party has announced a major rally in Visakhapatnam, aiming to garner support for their policies ahead of the upcoming elections. The rally is expected to draw thousands of attendees.

Opposition Leader Criticizes Government’s Policies

N. Chandrababu Naidu, leader of the opposition Telugu Desam Party (TDP), has strongly criticized the state government's policies, claiming they are detrimental to the state’s growth. He called for better governance and transparency.
